Welcome to Seventeen's World

-- List of Albums --

Seventeen's discography showcases their growth from energetic, youthful pop to more mature and experimental sounds. Starting with 17 Carat in 2015, they built a reputation for their tight choreography and self-produced music. Albums like An Ode (2019) and Attacca (2021) highlight their evolving musical complexity, blending genres like rock, EDM, and R&B. Hits like "Don't Wanna Cry," "Clap," and "Hot" demonstrate their ability to fuse emotional depth with high-energy performances. Through their diverse discography, Seventeen has become known for their musical versatility, emotional storytelling, and strong fan connection.

Semicolon

• It delivers an encouraging message to the youth, much like SEVENTEEN’s Heng æ. Using the semicolon as a metaphor, it invites young people to pause, breathe, and enjoy life. SEVENTEEN expresses empathy and hope, cheering on those who are navigating life’s struggles.

YOU MADE MY DAWN

• It is SEVENTEEN’s sixth mini album, released on January 21, 2019. The title evolved from their previous album, You Make My Day, as previewed in a midnight trailer. Happy Ending

• It is the first Japanese single by SEVENTEEN. It was released on May 29, 2019, with "Happy Ending" serving as the title track.


Always Yours

• It shares a heartfelt message of loyalty and support. The album’s themes revolve around commitment and staying by loved ones through all moments. Through uplifting tracks, SEVENTEEN reassures fans of their constant connection and appreciation.

Heaven

• Heaven by SEVENTEEN explores themes of love and aspiration, evoking a sense of warmth and hope. The album's tracks convey feelings of bliss and reaching for dreams, creating an uplifting experience for listeners. SEVENTEEN captures the joy of finding one’s “heaven” in life through meaningful lyrics and melodies.

Spill the feels

Maestro
